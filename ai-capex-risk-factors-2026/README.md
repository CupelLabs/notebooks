## How to verify

Every risk factor quote in the article is sourced from the 10-K filings linked above. Open the filing, Ctrl+F the quoted text. Capex guidance comes from Q4 2025 earnings calls, linked in the article methodology section.

## Limitations

- Risk factor language is selected, not comprehensive. Each filing contains dozens of risk factors; the article focuses on AI-specific language.
- Capex guidance uses midpoints of stated ranges.
- "No assurance" and similar phrases are common in SEC filings. The article's argument rests on the escalation of this language and its juxtaposition with spending levels, not the language alone.
